Payment Issues Q&A - সহায় প্রেগন্যান্সি অ্যাপ

Q1: How do I update my payment method?

Answer: To update your payment method, please go to the settings section in the app and select 'Payment Options'. You can then add or update your preferred payment methods including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and direct bank transfers...

Q2: What should I do if my payment fails?

Answer: In case of a payment failure, check your network connection and the details of your payment method. If the issue persists, contact our support team via email or our support chat. Make sure to provide details of the transaction for quicker assistance...
